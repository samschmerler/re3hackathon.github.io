Creative storytellers

Crucial stories deserve top talent.

We know you are really busy. We know you are really talented. We know you (might) find the typical hackathon a bit chaotic and ineffective, but we’re here to change that. This is a chance to work on something you feel passionate about, meet and work with talented collaborators, and make something meaningful.

If you're as excited as we are about this, tell us a little bit about yourself in the form below. Creative people of all stripes are welcome to apply. Just let us know what skills you can bring to the table and why this thing shouldn't happen without you.

In early September, we'll unlock invitations for 75 talented, committed, multidisciplinary story hackers, selected based on expertise, experience, work samples and interests. Story hackers will be organized into teams that will tackle one of ten important stories spanning a wide range of social issues. Selected stories will also be announced in early September.
